## 2. Internal identity model

Recommended architecture:
- **EchoMind** = intelligence framework and philosophy
- **Spektraxa** = intelligence identity / entity
- **AIR** = Automated Intelligence Responder, the user-facing handle

In practice, users do not need to understand the full internal stack. They only need a simple communication path.

Example invocation:
- `Hello AIR`

This keeps the ecosystem elegant: EchoMind is the broader intelligence environment, Spektraxa is the intelligent presence, and AIR is how people speak to it.

---

## 3. Why the AIR handle matters

Most people will not remember complex internal brand hierarchies. AIR gives the platform a simple and memorable interface name that can be used consistently across:
- homepage prompts
- onboarding flows
- investor demos
- education modules
- operator consoles
- metaverse / immersive interactions

A short invocation like `Hello AIR` lowers friction and makes the ecosystem feel approachable.

## 5. Functional role

Spektraxa / AIR should be positioned as an **intelligence and interaction layer**, not as the consensus engine or financial authority.

AIR may:
- explain products and workflows
- answer questions
- guide users through onboarding
- help users understand staking, borrowing, and cash-out
- assist operators and institutions with platform explanations
- attach or summarize policy-safe context

AIR may not:
- override consensus
- authorize final settlement by itself
- replace validator signatures
- bypass compliance or treasury policy

This separation aligns with the broader Veritas / SLVR8 architecture already outlined in the technical drafts, where AIR and PoM remain advisory or attestation-oriented rather than finality-critical. [Source](https://outthefreezer.square.site/whitepaper)

- helpful
